Duddingston Greenspace Survey

local1 Meadowfield Gardens Play Area

Features

Natural: Grass/Weeds, Scattered trees Artificial: fences, walls Exposure: South facing slope. Broken shade from trees Accessibility: No wheelchair access - impractical due to steps and unpaved surfaces. Could easily be improved.

History and Potential Uses

Unsure of history. Originally it was fields of an estate. Does not appear to be a 'planned space'. But may have been planned as a play area as part of the housing development on the site in the 1960-70's. Growing spaces, improved play area, wheelchair access 2 * play equipment - dishevelled. One child seat with spring base. One wooden revolving balance blank. No ball games Woodpeckers, Tree Creepers and Sparrowhawks seen in area.
