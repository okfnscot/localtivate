Locate+Cultivate

Ordinary maps tell us about streets, buildings and parks, but give little or no information about unused or abandoned spaces in our neighbourhood. Locate+Cultivate is an opportunity to discover these pockets of green space, put them on a map and think about ways of using them to help the community. This is a new initiative in Edinburgh, and we started in Duddingston.
